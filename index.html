Gabe Rosenfeld

Graphic Designer

Who Am I?

I am an all around creative person that loves to make! I am born, raised, and still live in Philly. I enjoy pop culture, hip-hop, fashion, and of course ART! I have been doing photography for 5 years, graphic design for 4 years, and videography for 2 years. I have created freelance work for many different company, brands, organizations, etc., including Bayada Home Health Care.

What Do I Make?

I create anything and everything that has to do with digital art and design. From logos to business cards to brochures to magazines, I love to apply my creativity and skills to all sorts of mediums. I mostly with startups and individuals, but love working with companies of all sizes.


Why Choose Me?

I create eye-catching graphics fast, and for a low price. My prices are just a fraction of big design firms, however my work is very comparable. I constantly communicate with my clients and allow them to give infinite revisions to my work. This ensures that clients are always satisfied with not only the end product, but also the process that got them there.
